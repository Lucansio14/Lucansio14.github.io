Research

Theses:

  • Bachelor’s Thesis / Final Degree Project: It centered around MHD waves in the solar corona. Specifically, it focused on studying the effect of resonant attenuation on transverse oscillations of radial harmonics of magnetohydrodynamic kink modes within solar coronal loops. You can check it out here (only in Spanish...)
  • Master’s Thesis: It focused on Alfvén waves in the solar photosphere. It specifically addressed the investigation of dissipative effects on Torsional Alfvén Waves (TAWs) in magnetic flux tubes, located in Bright Points (BPs) in the solar photosphere. You can visualize it here (in English)
