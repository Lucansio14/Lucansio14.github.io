Research

Theses:

  • Bachelor’s Thesis / Final Degree Project: It centered around MHD waves in the solar corona. Specifically, it focused on studying the effect of resonant attenuation on transverse oscillations of radial harmonics of magnetohydrodynamic kink modes within solar coronal loops.
    Supervisor: Roberto Soler (University of the Balearic Islands).
    [PDF] (in Spanish).
  • Master’s Thesis: It focused on Alfvén waves in the solar photosphere. It specifically addressed the investigation of dissipative effects on Torsional Alfvén Waves (TAWs) in magnetic flux tubes, located in Bright Points (BPs) in the solar photosphere.
    Supervisor: Roberto Soler (University of the Balearic Islands).
    [PDF] (in English).
